About

My name is Zeyd Uysal and as a student Applied Informatics I am passionate about computers with an emphasis on software development. Programming day in day out feels like being on a playground doing what I love most, my hobby Engineering, sharing knowledge, leading people and being able to set out a path to bring a project from start to end is what gets me out of bed in the morning and keeps me from going back into it. A relaxing vacation to me could be defined as a day where I can enjoy coding with my colleagues, do what I love most and not miss my gym appointment.

Whenever I'm not sitting behind my desk you can most probably find me drinking a cup of coffee either participating in a hackathon or challenging others love for programming. Swimming in a pool, being on my bike or taking a hike is what keeps my body and mind in shape. Other than the love for IT, books is where the paycheck usually goes to.
